Doner Company

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Doner Company (formerly W.B. Doner & Co.) is an American advertising agency headquartered in Southfield, Michigan. It was founded in 1937 by Wilfred Broderick Doner. It is the largest privately-owned advertising agency in the United States.[citation needed] The Doner Company also has a United Kingdom-based agency called Doner Cardwell Hawkins Company.

Among its clients, past and present, are:

  • Expedia - "Go With Confidence"
  • May Department Stores
  • Blockbuster
  • Timex - "Takes a licking and keeps on ticking"
  • Arby's - Oven Mitt
  • Six Flags - Mr. Six
  • ADT Security Services
  • Cox Communications - Digital Max
  • Sylvan Learning Center
  • Great Wolf Lodge
  • AAA
  • The UPS Store
  • HGTV
  • Zaditor
  • Detroit Zoo
  • PNC Financial Services
  • Serta
  • DuPont
  • Owens Corning
  • Online Vehicle Exchange
  • Hotels.com
  • Sherwin-Williams
  • Bush Brothers and Company (Bush's Beans)

  • Kwikset
