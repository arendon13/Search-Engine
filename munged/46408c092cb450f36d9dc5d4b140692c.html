Gretchen Lieberum is a Los Angeles- based jazz vocalist and song-writer.

Raised in Berkeley, California, she developed a passion for jazz, funk and urban beats within the Bay Area scene.

After fronting a funk band for several years, Lieberum released her first solo album, 3am (1998) followed by a record named Brand New Morning (2002), both produced by Greg Poree. Her latest album, Siren Songs (2005), was independently released in the United States.
