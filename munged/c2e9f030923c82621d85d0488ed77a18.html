May Bumps 2004

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The May Bumps 2004 were a set of rowing races held at Cambridge University from Wednesday June 9, 2004 until Saturday June 12, 2004. The event was run as a bumps race and is one of a series of May Bumps which have been held annually in mid-June since 1887. See May Bumps for the format of the races. In 2004, a total of 171 crews took part (94 men's crews and 77 women's crews), with around 1500 participants in total. Several thousand spectators came to watch, particularly on the Saturday.

[edit] Head of the River crews

Caius men rowed-over in 1st position, achieving the headship for the 6th time since 1998 (and 3rd consecutive headship).

Emmanuel women bumped Newnham and held off a two-day challenge from Caius to take their first headship since 1999.

[edit] Highest 2nd VIIIs

The highest men's 2nd VIII for the 2nd consecutive year was Caius II.

The highest women's 2nd VIII for the 2nd consecutive year was Emmanuel II.
