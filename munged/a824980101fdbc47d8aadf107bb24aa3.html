Steve Kinsey (athelete)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Steve Kinsey (born 2 January 1963, Manchester) was an English football forward who played for Manchester City F.C. between 1979 and 1986. He played 101 times for the team and scored 15 goals, including one at Wembley versus Chelsea in the first Full Members Cup Final, before transferring to Minnesota Strikers in the United States to play indoor football. He then played indoor with the Tacoma Stars, and in the APSL (outdoor) with the Fort Lauderdale Strikers. He still resides in the US today.
