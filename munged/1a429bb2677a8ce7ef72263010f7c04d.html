Mark Ettles

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Mark Ettles (born October 30, 1966 in Perth, Australia) is an Australian born baseball player who played for the San Diego Padres in 1993. He was a member of the Australia national baseball team, that ended up in sixth place at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney, Australia.
