Rene Garcia

Born July 10, 1974 (1974-07-10) (age 33)
Hialeah, Florida

Rene Garcia (born July 10, 1974 in Hialeah, Florida)is a member of the Florida House of Representatives. He is a graduate from Florida International University and is of Cuban parentage.

He served of the City of Hialeah City Council from 1997-2000.

Mr. Garcia currently represents District 110 as a Republican which consists mostly of Hialeah and Miami Lakes. He was first elected to the Florida House in 2000 and subsequently reelected. Garcia is constitutionally termed out of office in 2008.
