Juice 107.2 is an Independent Local Radio station based in Brighton, UK, playing MOR dance music and commercial classics. It also has a sister station - the internet radio site Totally Radio. Juice was formerly known as Surf 107.

They regularly feature the shows Unfold and Dugout, which feature DJs and artists from the Tru Thoughts independent record label.

In 2007, Guy & Andrea's programme was voted "The Best Drivetime Show in the South East of England" by a Media Website [1].
