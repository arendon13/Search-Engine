Mark McCoy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Mark McCoy is a current or former vocalist for several punk rock and Power Violence bands, some of which include Charles Bronson (band), Das Oath, and Holy Molar. He also recorded a spoken-word comedy split 7" with Mark Telfian of Limp Wrist.

Highly influential Power Violence band Spazz have a song titled "Hardcore Before Mark McCoy Was Emo Semen".
