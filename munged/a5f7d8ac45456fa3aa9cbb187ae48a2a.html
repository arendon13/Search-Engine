Serbian and Montenegrin National Badminton Championships

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Serbian and Montenegrin National Badminton Championships is a tournament organized to crown the best badminton players in Serbia and Montenegro. They are held since 2003, and were preceded by the Yugoslavian National Badminton Championships.
