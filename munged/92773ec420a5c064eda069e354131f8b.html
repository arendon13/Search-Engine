Nithin Sathya

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Nithin Sathya (born 9th April 1976), is an up-and-coming film actor in Kollywood, the Tamil film industry.

[edit] Career

He first appeared in supporting roles in several films. He became a noticeable actor in Chennai 600028, where he played a street cricket player.
