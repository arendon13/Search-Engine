New York State Route 228 is a state highway in western New York, USA. The highway is located in the Finger Lakes District. The road runs in a northeast to southwest direction with the northeastern end in Perry City at NY 227 and the southwestern end at NY 224 in the Village of Odessa. NY 228 is entirely within Schuyler County.
