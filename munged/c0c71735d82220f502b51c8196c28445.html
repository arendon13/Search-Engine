John Cheshire

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Air Chief Marshal Sir John Cheshire KBE CB (born 1942) was the Lieutenant Governor of the Bailiwick of Jersey, Channel Islands from 24 January 2001 to 7 April 2006, when his term of office ended. He was succeeded in the post by Lieutenant General Andrew Ridgway CB, CBE. ACM Sir John Cheshire was previously head of NATO Command Allied Forces-Northwest.
