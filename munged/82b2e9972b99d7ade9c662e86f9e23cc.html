Quarantine is a horror film due to be released 17 October 2008. It is a remake of the Spanish film REC.[1] Sony Pictures has launched a viral marketing campaign for the film. It was rated R by the Motion Picture Association of America for bloody violent and disturbing content, terror and language. [2]
