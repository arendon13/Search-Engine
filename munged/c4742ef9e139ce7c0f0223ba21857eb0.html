Leopold Wohlrab

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Men's handball
Silver 1936 Berlin Team competition

Leopold Wohlrab (born March 22, 1912 - died January 5, 1981) was an Austrian field handball player who competed in the 1936 Summer Olympics.

He was part of the Austrian field handball team, which won the silver medal. He played three matches.
