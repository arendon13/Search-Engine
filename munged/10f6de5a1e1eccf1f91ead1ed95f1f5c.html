Marcos Sanchez

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Marcos Sanchez is a basketball player from Miami, Florida. He went to college in duke and returned to miami in 2005 to play for the Miami Heat. He is 6'6" and plays guard and forward.

Marcos Sanchez is a Babalawo and Olorisa of Yemoja of puerto rican and panamanian descent born in Chicago. He is author of the Ifa site at http://ifalola.blogspot.com and has traveled extensively in his studies of the Orisa traditions both in Nigeria and the diaspora.
