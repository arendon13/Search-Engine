Piet Cleij

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Piet Cleij (born 27 May 1927, Groningen) is a Dutch linguist who lives in Bilthoven, Netherlands. He is currently the vice-secretary of the Union Mundial pro Interlingua and the chief lexicographer in the Interlingua community.
