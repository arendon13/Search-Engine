SANNI is a Kerala-based singer and was a contestant on Idea star singer.[1] He was a popular contestant in the musical reality show 'Idea star singer 2007' .

[edit] Idea Star singer 2007

Sanni is considered to be a very fine singer and used to be praised by all the judges.Sanni represented the common man among the other contestants.He is one of the people's favorites .A simple , humble & down to earth person, he has worked in 5 albums as the main singer.However, he was eliminated from Idea Star singer in January,2008.
