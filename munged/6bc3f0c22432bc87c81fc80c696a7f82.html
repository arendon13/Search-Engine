Zongshen/Kangda Pexma KD50QT-4 is a four stroke, single cylinder city-scooter, with an engine displacement of 50C.C.

Specifications:

Engine type: 4-stroke, one-cylinder, air-cooled, OHC, 2 valves.

Displacement: 49,7 C.C.

Horsepower: 3,4 Bhp (2,5 Kw)

Fuel economy: 54 km/l

Problems:

  • The drivetrain, a belt at the size of 669-18-30, has a tendense to wear very quikly.
  • The electric starter, has problems working.
  • The plastic is of a poor quality, and may breake very easily.
  • The tyres are made of a very thin nylon, and have a tendense to flat.
  • The rear brake, is not good and barely even works.
