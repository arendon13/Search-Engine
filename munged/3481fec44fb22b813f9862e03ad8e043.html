Forehill Primary is a primary school in Bridge of Don, Aberdeen, a city in Scotland. It opened in 1982 and has taught children of ages 4- 12 since then. When it was made it had only 50 pupils and 4 staff, but now there is a total of 250 pupils and 17 staff. The current headteacher is Mrs. Moore, and the depute head is Mrs. Harris.

The building is a single story school with open plan classes situated in two main teaching areas around a central courtyard.

It is one of the five primary schools that lies in the Oldmachar Academy catchment area, the others being Danestone, Glashieburn, Greenbrae and Middleton Park Primary Schools.

Forehill has just celebrated its 25th birthday.
