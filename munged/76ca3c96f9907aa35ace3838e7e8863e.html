Barbara Seranella

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Barbara Seranella (30 April 1956 - 21 January 2007) was an American author.

[edit] Early life

Barbara was born in Santa Monica, California and then grew up in Pacific Palisades but ran away at 14 after a terrible childhood. She then joined a hippie commune but gave it up to become a auto mechanic.

[edit] Death

Barbara Seranella died in Cleveland Clinic in Cleveland, Ohio. She was waiting for a Liver Transplant to cure her Liver disease. Her husband Ron Seranella and brother Dr. Larry Shore. She died peacefully on January 21, 2007, at 4:15 p.m. EST (1:15 p.m. PST).
