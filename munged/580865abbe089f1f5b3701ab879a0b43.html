Olinda Joy Elisabet Borggren (b. September 30, 1976) is a Swedish television personality who was a contestant on Joe Millionaire and the first season of the Swedish version of Paradise Hotel. She is also a singer and a model.

[edit] Career

She became romantically involved with another contestant on Paradise Hotel, Patrick Michael Larsson, and they have two new specials on TV4's sister channel TV400 after their Paradise Hotel visit.

One special was named The OP Tour; in it, Borggren and Larsson showed how their music show was produced, they travelled all over Sweden, and Borggren sang her single "Playboy Bunny", which was the most sold mobile phone ringtone of 2005.

Their next show was Little Italy, where Borggren and Larsson went to Italy and met different Italian celebrities, such as the football player Maldini. The show aired on TV400.

Borggren and Larsson have also been hosts for the talk show Paradise Hotel Extra, for the cast members of season two, where they met once a week and discussed the intrigues of the week.

[edit] American television

Borggren also appeared in the second season of the American reality show Joe Millionaire, where she was cast as one of the women who competed for Joe. Borggren moved back home to Sweden after the show ended, and was quickly cast for Paradise Hotel.

Borggren and Larsson now live in Marbella, Spain, where they make many clips for their homepage, The Official Oplounge Blog and tell their fans about what they are doing. They have no plans to return to Sweden any time soon, according to their homepage.
