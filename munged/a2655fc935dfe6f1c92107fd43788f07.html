Pyrenaearia
Scientific classification
Kingdom: Animalia

Class: Gastropoda
Order: Stylommatophora
Family: Hygromiidae
Genus: Pyrenaearia

Pyrenaearia is a genus of gastropod in the Hygromiidae family. It contains the following species:

  • Pyrenaearia cotiellae
  • Pyrenaearia daanidentata
  • Pyrenaearia molae
  • Pyrenaearia navasi
  • Pyrenaearia oberthueri
  • Pyrenaearia organiaca
  • Pyrenaearia parva
  • Pyrenaearia poncebensis
  • Pyrenaearia velascoi
