CEWC-Cymru

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

CEWC-Cymru (Council for Education in World Citizenship-Wales) is an educational charity working with young people to promote active global citizenship in Wales.

Established in the 1940s, CEWC-Cymru is based at the Temple of Peace, Cardiff, but works with schools, colleges and youth groups throughout Wales.

CEWC-Cymru receives its core funding from the Welsh Assembly Government, local education authorities, and over 100 school and college members. Julian Hodge Bank sponsors its annual debating competition.
