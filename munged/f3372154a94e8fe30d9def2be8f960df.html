Dick Vincent

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Dick Vincent (b. 1939 in Kirkland Lake, Ontario) was best known as the announcer and on-air host at KCND-TV in the 1960s and 1970s.

Prior to that he was station announcer at CJOB,[1], from 1959 to 1960. Dick started his broadcasting career as a news reporter with radio station CKDM in Dauphin, Manitoba.[2] After KCND signed off and moved to Winnipeg, Manitoba as CKND in 1975, he moved with the station to Winnipeg, remaining as a station personality and hosting local programs including Town 'n Country.[3]
