Robert Benjamin Schmidt (April 22, 1933 in St. Louis, Missouri), is a former professional baseball player who played catcher in the Major Leagues from 1958-1965. He played for the San Francisco Giants, Washington Senators, Cincinnati Reds, and New York Yankees.
