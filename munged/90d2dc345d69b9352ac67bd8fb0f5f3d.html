Campbellford District High School is located at 960 - 199 Ranney Street North in Campbellford, Ontario, Canada. As of 2003 it was home to 702 students and 42 teachers. It is part of the Kawartha Pine Ridge District School Board.

Secretary: Laurie Johnson
