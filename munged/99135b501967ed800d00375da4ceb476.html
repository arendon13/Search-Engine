Walter D'Souza

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Men's field hockey
Gold 1948 London Team

Walter Luis D'Sousa (born December 16, 1920) was an Indian field hockey player.

[edit] Archivements

Year Milestone
1920 Born on December 16 in M'Bale, East Africa
1943 Joined the Lusitanian Hockey Team
  • Won the Provincial Cup
  • Won the Lewis Cup
  • Runners-Up in the Aga Khan Trophy
1943 Joined the Young Goans Football Club
  • Won the Nadkarni Cup
1944 - 1945 Captained the Lusitanians
  • Won the Aga Khan Trophy
  • Runners-Up in the Lewis Cup
1944 & 1945 Young Goans Football Club
  • Won the Nadkarni Cup in both years
1947 - 1948 Represented India
  • East Africa Tour
1948 Played in the 1948 London Olympics
1949 Played in the Jashan Celebrations in Afghanistan
