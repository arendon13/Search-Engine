Maul may refer to:

  • Maul (hammer), a heavy long-handled hammer, usually with an oversized wooden head.
  • Splitting maul, a hammer similar to a sledgehammer but with one end of the head sharpened for splitting wood.
  • Maul (Rugby Union), a rugby term
  • Darth Maul, the Star Wars character
  • Maul (Wildstorm), a comic book character
  • Maul, the act of causing severe bodily injuries, often in relation to an animal attack (e.g., a bear attack)
  • The Mauls, a species of pig-like creatures in the Myth computer game series
  • Maul, a tower defense-like multiplayer gaming type in the strategy game Warcraft III.
