Martins Dukurs

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Martins Dukurs (born March 31, 1984) is a Latvian skeleton racer who has competed since 1998. He finished seventh in the men's skeleton event at the 2006 Winter Olympics in Turin.

Dukurs also finished sixth in the men's skeleton event at the 2007 FIBT World Championships in St. Moritz.
