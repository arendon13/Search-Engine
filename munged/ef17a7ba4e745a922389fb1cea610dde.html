Mark Jacoby (born May 21, 1947 in Johnson City, Tennessee, USA) is a Broadway performer. He has achieved fame from his leading roles in, most notably, The Phantom of the Opera and Ragtime.

[edit] Biography

Mark Jacoby was born in Johnson City, Tennessee on May 21, 1947.

He married Deborah Bendixen. They have two children, Ben and Evelyn, who grew up in Maplewood, New Jersey and attended Columbia High School.
