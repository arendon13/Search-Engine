Time Is Fiction is an Edison Glass album produced by Nathan Dantzler and released by Credential Recordings on February 5th, 2008, after being pushed back from a scheduled November 20th, 2007 release date. The CD release show was held at Looney Tunes CD Store in West Babylon, New York, the same location where the band held the CD release show for their debut album, A Burn or a Shiver.
