Super B is a Greek local radio and television station serving all of the Achaia prefecture. Its headquarters are in Patras. It offers movies as well as local programs and programs as well as music from Greece, North America including the United States, Australia and the British Isles.

[edit] Logo and slogan

Its slogan used to be an upside-down golden triangle which was used in the early to mid 1990s, its logo was dropped later on and was only written in letters, in the 2000s, the letter text was changed.

Super B is also an amateur video game. it is playable at Newgrounds.com. It stars a superhero named Super B(Box).
