[edit] PrefixNE

PrefixNE is a PC management software product by Prefix IT Ltd.


[edit] Lateset Version

On September 2007, Prefix IT Ltd has released version 3.5.


[edit] Features

  • Policy Enforcer
  • Alert Bar
  • Act and Deploy
  • Surgical Threat Removal
  • Inventory and Assests Manager
  • Report Generator
  • Urgent Assistance
