David Chambers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other persons named David Chambers, see David Chambers (disambiguation).

David Chambers is a British economist. He earned an MA from Oxford, an MA from Cornell and an MS and a PhD from Carnegie Mellon University. Chambers is currently emeritus professor of comparative management at London Business School.
