Junaid Hartley

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Junaid Hartley (born 22 June 1978) is a South African football (soccer) player. A midfielder, Junaid Hartley was a former player for Sarawak FA in the Malaysian League. He is a former South Africa national football team players from 1997-1999, and he also played for South Africa national football team in 1997 FIFA World Youth Championship in Malaysia.

[edit] Clubs

  • 1994-1997 : Wits University
  • 1997-1998 : Vitoria FC Setubal
  • 1997-1999 : Racing Club de Lens
  • 2000-2001 : Orlando Pirates
  • 2001-2002 : Moroka Swallows
  • 2002-2003 : Ajax Cape Town
  • 2003-2004 : Jomo Cosmos
  • 2004-2005 : Maritzburg United
  • 2005-2006 : Tembisa Classic
  • 2006-2007 : Sarawak FA
