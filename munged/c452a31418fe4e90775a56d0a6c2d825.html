Dharmavarapu Subramanyam
Born Dharmavarapu Subramanyam
August 9, 1960 (1960-08-09) (age 47)
Komminenivari Palem, Ballikuruva Mandalam, Prakasam District, Andhra Pradesh, India
Other name(s) Dharmavarapu

Dharmavarapu Subramanyam is an actor from the Telugu film industry. His roles usually are to provide comic relief to the audiences.He is chairman of Andhra Pradesh Cultural Association.
