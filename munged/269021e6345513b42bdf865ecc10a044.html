East Brooklyn Congregations (often abbreviated EBC) is a community-based organization serving several neighborhoods in New York City. It is affiliated with the Industrial Areas Foundation and headed by Michael Gecan. Among housing and public works projects, it has been involved in the formation of three public New York City high schools: EBC East New York, EBC Bushwick High School for Public Service, and Bushwick Leaders' High School for Academic Excellence.
