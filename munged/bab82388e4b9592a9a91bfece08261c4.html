Marty Allen (financier)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Marty Allen was the chairman, co-founder and largest individual stockholder (2,272,866 shares) of Computervision Corp.

Allen netted $34 million when, at age 57, he sold the company to Prime Computer Inc. on 29 January 1988.
