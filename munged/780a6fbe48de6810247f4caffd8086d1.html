State Route 610 is a north-south section line road in the northern Las Vegas area. It covers a northern section of Lamb Boulevard.

[edit] Route description

State Route 610 begins at Las Vegas Boulevard North (SR 604). It follows Lamb Boulevard north to its terminus at Interstate 15.

The state has plans to abandon this road from the state highway system.[1]
