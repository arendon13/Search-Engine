Donald Eaton Carr

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Donald Eaton Carr (1903 - ? ) is an American journalist, author and research chemist.

He was born in Los Angeles, California, on 17 October 1903. He obtained a science degree from University of California, Berkeley in 1930. In 1934 he married Mildred Clarke, From 1930 until 1947 he was a research chemist at the Union Oil Company. He authored seven books, mostly about air and water pollution.
