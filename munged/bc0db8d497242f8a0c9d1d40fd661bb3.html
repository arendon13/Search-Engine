Elephant Elephant
EP by Evelyn Evelyn
Released Sept. 13, 2007
Recorded 2007
Genre Americana / Showtunes
Label Eleven Records
Producer Amanda Palmer
Jason Webley

Elephant Elephant is an EP by Evelyn Evelyn. It was first sold to the public on Sept. 13, 2007 and consists of a 3 song 7" single and a 6 track "bonus" CD.

"Elephant Elephant" is the same on both the CD and vinyl. "Have You Seen My Sister Evelyn?" appears online and as part of "Goodnight Evelyn" in an alternate form compared to the vinyl release. The vinyl version has full fidelity while the online and CD versions sounds like an old-time radio transcription, even though it's just the studio version run through filters on a computer. All other tracks are exclusive to their particular formats.
