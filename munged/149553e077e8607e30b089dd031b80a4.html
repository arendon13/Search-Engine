Geoffrey Gurrumul Yunupingu

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Geoffrey Gurrumul Yunupingu (37 years) is an Australian musician (singer and guitar), that sings in yolngu language. He was born on Elcho Island and is from the Gumatj clan of the Yolngu. He is blind.

First solo album: Gurrumul — that jumped No. 1 on the iTunes Australia roots music chart (currently No. 3)[1]
