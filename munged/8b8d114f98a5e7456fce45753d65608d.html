Stenberg

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Stenberg is a surname that was the 11,945th most common last name in the United States as of the 2000 census.[1]

  • Don Stenberg, the former Attorney General of Nebraska
  • Jeremy Stenberg, American freestyle Motocross rider
  • Georgii and Vladimir Stenberg, Russian artists and designers
  • Kaj Stenberg, Swedish actor
  • Ragnar Stenberg, Finnish athlete
  • Rolf Stenberg, Finnish mathematician
  • Lars Lillo-Stenberg, Norwegian musician
  • Per Lillo-Stenberg, Norwegian actor

[edit] See also

  • Stenberg v. Carhart, a Supreme Court case that ruled laws against partial birth abortions to be illegal
  • Stenberg College, a Canadian health care education facility.
