The Fairly OddParents (season 7)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

After a year long hiatus, the show was revived for a Seventh Season to contain 20 episodes. The first 4 episodes aired March 10 - 13, 2008 as part of "Poof! There He Is! Week". The next 5 episodes aired from May 12 - 16, as part of "Turner & Poof Week". One episode titled "King Chang" currently does not have a "partner episode" which has aired ("The End of the Universe-ity"), thus making 83½ episodes.

78-79 "Fairly OddBaby"  Monday, February 18, 2008 701/702

When Timmy wishes up a baby for Cosmo and Wanda to fulfill their never-ending desire to be parents, the Anti-Fairies and the Pixies join forces to use the baby's untapped magic to take over the world. It's up to Timmy, Cosmo, Wanda, and Jorgen to make sure the first fairy baby born in 10,000 years is safe with its new family. The hour-long special episode guest stars Mary Hart as Fairy Hart and Ben Stein returning as HP & Sanderson.
First Appearance: Poof

Vicky's Head Gag: Baby Bottle 
80 "Mission: Responsible/Hairicane"  Monday, March 10 - Tuesday, March 11, 2008 703

Mission: Responsible Cosmo and Wanda are exhausted from taking care of Poof, so Timmy volunteers to take over and give them a night on the town. The minute they poof away, Timmy loses Poof.
Hairicane: Timmy wishes for an indestructible luscious hairstyle so his dad doesn't give him anymore haircuts. Wanda brings Poof to the barber, Dr. Ripstudwell for a haircut, but Poof is way too resistant to the barber.

Vicky's Head Gag: Dr. Ripstudwell 
81 "Open Wide And Say Aggh!/OddPirates"  Wednesday, March 12 - Thursday, March 13, 2008 704

Open Wide And Say Aggh!: Timmy needs to go to the hospital to have his tonsils removed. And his candy striper nurse is Vicky, who's determined to make his stay in the hospital as painful as possible!
OddPirates: Timmy wishes to go to the Dimmsdale Pirates baseball game, but Poof accidentally wishes Timmy, Cosmo, Wanda, and himself on a real Pirate ship, and the parrot on Dirtybeard steals Cosmo and Wanda's stars on their wands.

Vicky's Head Gag: Crossbones 
82 "The Odd Squad/For Emergencies Only"  Monday, May 12, - Tuesday, May 13, 2008 705

Odd Squad: When Timmy wishes for a talking car, he goes on an adventure in Fairy World with Wanda, Poof and "Carsmo". This leads to them soon believing Mama Cosma is missing.
For Emergencies Only: Feeling that Cosmo and Wanda are neglecting him for Poof, Timmy convinices his godparents to give him his own magic wand with ten wishes on it for emergencies.

Vicky's Head Gag: TV 
83 "Land Before Timmy/Cheese and Crockers"  Wednesday, May 14, - Thursday, May 15, 2008 706

Land Before Timmy: When Timmy wishes for a simpler world, everyone is transported to prehistoric times, and when volcanoes hit prehistorical Dimmsdale, Timmy has to find a way to power Cosmo and Wanda's wands before the city burns down.
Cheese and Crockers: To celebrate half day, Timmy wishes for a machine that allows things to merge with other items to become half-human and half-something else. When Crocker gets a hold of it, he accidentally merges himself with cheese, and he uses this power to capture Timmy's fairies.

Vicky's Head Gag: Cheese
