Experience Gloria Gaynor
Studio album by Gloria Gaynor
Released 1975
Genre Disco, R&B
Label MGM Records M3G-4997
Gloria Gaynor chronology
Never Can Say Goodbye (Gloria Gaynor album) Experience Gloria Gaynor I've Got You

Gloria Gaynor's successful second album, Experience Gloria Gaynor, was released on MGM Records in 1975. This album picks up where her debut left off.

Side one of the album is dance-oriented, while side two is R&B/Soul music oriented. Includes the hit singles; "Casanova Brown", "(If You Want It) Do It Yourself" and "How High The Moon" again presented in a 19 minute disco suite which club DJ's went crazy for. The Soul music songs on side two of the album are equally as impressive. The standout songs on side two are "What'll I Do", an excellent rendition of the Dionne Warwick hit, "Walk On By" and the self penned ballad, "I'm Still Yours."

The album was released on CD in 2005 by the Japanese label P-Vine.
