Like many other Christian and Muslim adherents, members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints believe that there will be a Second Coming of Jesus Christ to the earth sometime in the future. The LDS Church and its leaders do not make any predictions of the actual date of the Second Coming.

According to LDS Church teachings, Mormonism will be taught in all parts of the world prior to the Second Coming. It is also believed that there will be increasingly severe earthquakes, hurricanes, and other natural disasters prior to the Second Coming.

[edit] Signs of the times

Latter-day Saints use the term "signs of the times" to refer to events that are to occur prior to the Second Coming. According to one Latter-day Saint commentator,[1] there are many such signs, many of which have already passed.

  • Discovery and use of printing
  • Protestant reformation and age of renaissance
  • Discovery and colonization of America
  • Establishment of the American nation
  • Translation and printing of the Bible
  • Establishment of U.S. Constitution
  • Latter-day revelation

  • Opposition to the Book of Mormon
  • Restoration of keys and priesthood

  • Church and kingdom set up again
  • Growth of the church

  • Ten tribes to return
  • Times of Gentiles being fulfilled

  • Building of latter-day temples
  • Lord to come suddenly to temple
  • Spirit of Elijah and genealogical research

  • Persecution of the Jews

  • Worldly knowledge to increase
  • Scientific and inventive progress
  • Disease, plague, pestilence to sweep earth

  • Disasters and calamaties to abound
  • Strikes, anarchy, violence to increase

  • Holy Spirit ceasing to strive with wicked
  • Peace taken from earth
  • Angels now reaping the earth
  • Wars and rumors of wars
  • Famines, depressions, and economic turmoil
  • Apostate darkness covers earth

  • The gathering at Adam-ondi-Ahman

  • Fall of the great and abominable church
