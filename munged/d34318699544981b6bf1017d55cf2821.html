Tommaso Bona

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tommaso Bona (second half 16th century) was an Italian painter of the Renaissance period, mainly active in Brescia.

He painted a Magdalen for the church of the Annunziata in Brescia. In 1577, along with Pietro Marone he painted the nave of the "Duomo Vecchio" of San Pietro. He also painted in the Sala del Consiglio of the Palazzo della Loggia (signed 18 July 1688).
