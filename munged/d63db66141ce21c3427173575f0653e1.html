Sigvard Nilsson-Thurneman, Sven Emil Sigvard Nilsson-Thurneman (born November 14, 1908 in Sala, Sweden. died July 11, 1979 in Bromma, Sweden) was the leader of the gang Sala gang (Salaligan), who were active in Sala in the first 1930's. The league/gang murdered 5 people and robbed them and was eventually caught.

Sigvard Thurneman's followers did everything he said and he turned them into murderers. Sigvard's view of life and death and morals was completely cold. He said it himself that it was nothing wrong in killing people, as death only meant a radical change in the person's physical condition.
