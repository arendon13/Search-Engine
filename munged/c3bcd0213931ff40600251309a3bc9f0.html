Jimmy Patronis (born April 13, 1972) in Panama City, Florida.

Patronis is a Representative in the House of Representatives of the U.S. state of Florida. He received his Bachelors degree from the Florida State University in 1996. It should also be noted he lives in Panama City Beach, Florida with his family.
