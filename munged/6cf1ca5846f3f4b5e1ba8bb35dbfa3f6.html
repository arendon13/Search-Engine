Smokestack is a 1963 studio album by jazz pianist Andrew Hill released by the Blue Note Records label (his second recording as leader on the record label).[1]

[edit] Track listing

  1. Smokestack
  2. Day After
  3. Wailing Wall
  4. Ode to Von
  5. Not So
  6. Verne
  7. 30 Pier Avenue

[edit] Personnel

  • Andrew Hill - piano
  • Richard Davis - double-bass
  • Eddie Khan - double-bass
  • Roy Haynes - drums
