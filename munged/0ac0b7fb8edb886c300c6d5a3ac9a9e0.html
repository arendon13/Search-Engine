Yvonne Monlaur (December 15, 1939 - ) is a retired French film actress of the late 1950s and 1960s best known for her roles in the Hammer horror films.

She starred in the 1958 Italian film Three Strangers in Rome which was amongst Claudia Cardinale's earliest films and in 1960 in the horror film Circus of Horrors alongside prominent actors in British film such as Anton Diffring and Donald Pleasance. In 1960 she also starred in the Hammer horror film The Brides of Dracula alongside other noted British actors of the day Peter Cushing and Freda Jackson.

Monlaur screentested for the role of Domino Derval 1965 James Bond film Thunderball. The role eventually went to another French actress, Claudine Auger.[1].
