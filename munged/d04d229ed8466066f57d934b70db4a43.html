Tommy Tutone 2

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tommy Tutone 2
Studio album by Tommy Tutone
Released 1981
Recorded ?
Genre Rock
Label Columbia

Tommy Tutone 2 is the second album of the rock group, Tommy Tutone. It featured the band's biggest hit, "867-5309/Jenny"

In October 2006, VH1 listed " 867-5309/Jenny" as the 36th greatest song of the 1980s. VH1: 100 Greatest Songs of the 80's


[edit] Track listing (1981 original issue - Columbia 37401)

Side 1

  1. "867-5309/Jenny"
  2. "Baby It's Alright"
  3. "Shadow on the Road Ahead"
  4. "Bernadiah"
  5. "Why Baby Why"

Side 2

  1. "Which Man Are You"
  2. "No Way to Cry"
  3. "Steal Away"
  4. "Tonight"
  5. "Only One"
  6. "Not Say Goodbye"

[edit] Personnel

  • Tommy Heath- Lead Vocals, Rhythm Guitar, Piano
  • Jim Keller- Lead Guitar, Vocals
  • Jon Lyons- Bass
  • Victor Carberry- Drums

[edit] Additional Musicians

  • Steve LeGassick- Keyboards (became an official member of the band in 1983)
  • Rick Cutler- Drums
  • Lonnie Turner- Bass
  • John Cowsill- Percussion, Harmony Vocals
  • Sam Clayton- Percussion
