Ainars Podnieks

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ainars Podnieks (born October 13, 1980) is a Latvian bobsledder who competed from 2003 to 2007. His best Bobsleigh World Cup finish was second in the four-man event at Lake Placid in December 2006.

Podnieks also finished seventh in the four-man event at the 2007 FIBT World Championships in St. Moritz.
