Tommaso Lequio di Assaba (born August 21, 1893 - died April 10, 1975) was an Italian horse rider who competed in the 1924 Summer Olympics.

In 1924 he and his horse Pimplo won the bronze medal in the team eventing competition, after finishing eleventh in the individual eventing.
