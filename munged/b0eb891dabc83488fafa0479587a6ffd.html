Chainpur Situated 11 km west of Bhabua headquarters. The fort at Chainpur is a structure of Suri or Akbar period. The Hindu shrine of Harsu Brahm is famous in the locality. It is said that Kanyalkubya priest of Raja Shaliwahan, named Harshu Pandey, ended his life in protest against demolition of his house but during his cremation was seen standing at the cremation site at Varanasi.

[edit] 'Harsu Pandey'

is said to become Brahm after his cremation.He was an avatar of Lord Shiva.The person who are disturbed by the devil or ghost are enshrined in Harsu's darbaar.People from far away like from U.P. ,Surguja(Chhattisgarh),Bihar,Jharkhand etc. come to his darbaar.It is believed that Harsu fulfills everybody wills and wishes.
