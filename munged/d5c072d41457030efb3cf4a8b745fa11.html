Bryan Little (baseball)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Richard Bryan "Twig" Little (born October 8, 1959 in Houston, Texas) was an infielder in Major League Baseball.

[edit] Teams

  • Montreal Expos 1982-1984
  • Chicago White Sox 1985-1986(start)
  • New York Yankees 1986 (end)
