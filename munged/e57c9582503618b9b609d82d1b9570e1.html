Beaton is a surname and may refer to:

  • Cecil Beaton (1904-1980), English photographer
  • David Cardinal Beaton (1494-1545)
  • James Beaton (1473-1539), Archbishop of Glasgow
  • James Beaton II, Archbishop of Glasgow
  • James Beaton (born 1943), Queen's Police Officer

  • Mary Beaton, attendant of Mary I of Scotland
