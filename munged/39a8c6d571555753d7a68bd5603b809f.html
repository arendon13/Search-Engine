The Machine Shop

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Machine Shop is a rock music venue located on Dort Highway in the south east side of Flint, Michigan. The club opened in 2002 and features national, regional, and local acts. It is owned and operated by brothers Kevin and Craig Zink.
