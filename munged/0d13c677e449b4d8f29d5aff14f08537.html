List of Florida Agricultural and Mechanical University alumni

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Florida Agricultural and Mechanical University

This is a list of notable alumni which includes graduates, non-graduate former students, and current students of Florida Agricultural and Mechanical University .

Florida A & M University is a public, four-year, historically black university located in Tallahassee, Florida. It is one of eleven institutions in Florida's State University System.

This is an incomplete list, which may never be able to satisfy certain standards for completeness.
Revisions and sourced additions are welcome.
