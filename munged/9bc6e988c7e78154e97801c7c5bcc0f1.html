Dave Bustion

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

David C. "Dave" Bustion, also known as Stretch (born August 30, 1949 in Gadsden, Alabama) is a retired American basketball player.

He played collegiately for the University of Denver.

He was selected by the KC-Omaha Kings in the 5th round (68th pick overall) of the 1972 NBA Draft.

He played for the Denver Rockets (1972-73) in the ABA for 47 games.
