Tom Schanley

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tom Schanley is an American actor, best known for his roles on television.

His credits include: The Yellow Rose, Fame, T.J. Hooker, Dynasty, Murder She Wrote, Baywatch, Melrose Place, JAG, ER, CSI: Miami, Star Trek: Enterprise and CSI: New York.
