Purina Farms is a corporate farm owned by animal-feed maker Purina in Gray Summit, Missouri, about 45 miles southwest of St. Louis, Missouri and 10 minutes away from Six Flags St. Louis. The farm is open to the public and features exhibits of domestic farm animals, a petting zoo, dog obedience training, and classrooms, as well as a tour tractor, and a dog show demonstration.
