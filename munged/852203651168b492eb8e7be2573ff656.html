Andrew Crews born on the 18th of August 1973 in Sydney, Australia, was a professional Australian Football (soccer) goalkeeper, who last played for the Marconi Stallions before retiring in 2006.

Crews started playing football for junior club Blacktown City and then later played for the first team in the NSW state legue before joining NSL team Sydney United in 1995. He went on to play for other NSL clubs Parramatta Power and Wollongong City as well as NSW state league clubs Leichhardt Tigers, Northern Tigers, St George and Marconi Stallions. In 2006 Crews was signed on a short-term contract by Perth Glory as a replacement player, following an injury to regular Perth Glory keeper Jason Petkovic. Crews played in the A-League Pre-Season Cup before leaving the club in September 2006.

[edit] Honours

Crews represented Australia in 2005 at the FIFA Beach Soccer World Cup.
