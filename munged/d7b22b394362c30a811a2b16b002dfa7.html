Vomit Pigs were a Punk band from Daingerfield, Texas. Their debut EP, Take One, was released in 1978.

The founding members of the Vomit Pigs were Mike Brock ("Mite Vomit"), Chris Connor, Doug Spencer, Roy Dalby and Eddie McMinn.

The EP Take One was recorded in Texarkana, Texas in a small studio owned by Sam Walton of Wal-Mart fame. Only 500 copies of the EP were pressed.[citation needed]
