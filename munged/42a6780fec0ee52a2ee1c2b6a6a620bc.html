Rainbow Water Snake or Striped Water Snake Enhydris enhydris is a species of snake.

Distribution:SE China,Indonesia (Bangka, Belitung, Java, Kalimantan, Sulawesi, Sumatra, We); ,Bangladesh; Cambodia; C/E India; Laos; ,Malaysia (Malaya and East Malaysia, Borneo, Pulau Tioman; ,Myanmar (Burma); Nepal; Pakistan; ,Singapore (?); Sri Lanka; Pulau Bangka, ,Thailand; Vietnam

Type locality: "Indiae orientalis"
