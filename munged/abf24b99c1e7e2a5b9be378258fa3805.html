Greg Lisher

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

 This article does not cite any references or sources. (September 2006)
Please help improve this article by adding citations to reliable sources. Unverifiable material may be challenged and removed.

Greg Lisher is the lead guitar player for Camper Van Beethoven, from 1986 until 1990, and again from 2003 to present.

He was also the lead guitarist for the Monks of Doom, from 1986 until 1993.

Lisher also has produced two solo albums.
