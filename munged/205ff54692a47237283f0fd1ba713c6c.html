Sean Raspet

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sean Raspet (born 1981) is an artist based in Brooklyn.

Raspet was born in Washington DC. He was raised in Jacksonville Florida where he frequented a country and western themed McDonalds. He makes a installations which are concerned with the relationship between objects, images and information.[1] His work is informed by philosophy and metaphysics.[2]
