Stewart School, Cuttack, Orissa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Stewart School is a very famous school located at Mission Road, Cuttack, Orissa, India.It was Established in the year 1882, when there were a handful of good schools in India. It was founded by Dr. William Stewart, a noted name in the department of health, Government of Orissa. Starting from a few rooms it has grown immensely in size. Apart from the school there is a college named Stewart Science College. It has continued its glorious journey from that point onwards, producing numerous gems, like Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose-responsible for the creation of Indian National Army, for the freedom of India.
