DiscoveryBox

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

DiscoveryBox is a children's magazine by Bayard Presse. It is targeted for children 9 to 12 years old. Inside there are topics about science, animals, history and world. It also includes games and quizzes. Its is designed for the completely independent reader and is the 3rd and final instalment of the Box series (after StoryBox and AdventureBox).

DicoveryBox is mostly non fictional and is designed to answer questions and expand the knowledge of its readers in the subjects that it covers each month.

There is a current shortage in this type of information rich magazine for this age group at the moment and children find the magazine very interesting. It is designed to build on what they have learned in School and it takes many of its subjects from the British Curriculum so reinforces what they have learned as well as adding additional interesting facts that they may not have previously known about.

Due to the fact there is a shortage of information magazines for children this age both ESL and English speaking students like to read this book as the information is specially presented for them. As it is specifically designed for the ages 9 to 12 the magazine takes subjects that they would find interesting such as The Olympic Games, Space Exploration and Avalanches being just a few of the previous topics covered.
