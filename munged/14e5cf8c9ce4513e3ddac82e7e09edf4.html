Deltoblastus

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Deltoblastus
Fossil range: Permian
Scientific classification
Kingdom: Animalia
Phylum: Echinodermata
Subphylum: Blastozoa
Class: Blastoidea
Order: Spiraculata
Genus: Deltoblastus

Deltoblastus is a genus of blastoid echinoderm that lived in the Permian. Its fossils are known from Asia.
