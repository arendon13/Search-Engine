Sherwood Park Mall is a shopping mall located in the Edmonton, Alberta, Canada suburb of Sherwood Park. It contains 94 stores and services including Zellers, Safeway, Galaxy 10 Cinemas, SportChek and Chapters.

[edit] Anchors

  • Zellers (115,713 sq. ft.)
  • Safeway (50,856 sq. ft.)
  • Galaxy 10 Cinemas (40,000 sq. ft.)
  • Strathcona Public Library (30,536 sq. ft.)
