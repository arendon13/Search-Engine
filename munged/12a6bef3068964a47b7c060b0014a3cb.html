[edit] Badgers in literature

  • Badger in the children novel The Animals of Farthing Wood and its components, Shadow in The Fox Cub Bold and Frond in Battle for the Park, all by Colin Dann.
  • Mr. Badger in The Wind in the Willows
  • The various badgers in the Redwall series.

[edit] Badgers in animated movies and TV series

  • Badger Moles in Avatar: The Last Airbender
  • Lacey Badger in My Gym Partner's a Monkey
  • Michelle, and her uncle Cornelius in Once Upon a Forest
  • Badger from Bodger and Badger

[edit] Badgers in games

  • The Pokémon Linoone resembles a badger.
  • The Blue Badger from the Ace Attorney series is the Police Force's mascot.
