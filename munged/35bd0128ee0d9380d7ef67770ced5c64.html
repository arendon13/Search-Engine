S. Ramachandra

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

S. Ramachandra (not S. Ramachandran) won National Film Award for Best Cinematography in 1977 for the Kannada feature film Rishya Shringa.

He has also won the Lifetime Achievement Award (at the State Film Awards) given by the Karnataka State Government in the year 2006.
