Institut de pastorale des Dominicains

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Institut de pastorale des Dominicains is the Montreal, Quebec branch of the Dominican University College / Collège Universitaire Dominicain, a Roman Catholic university based in Ottawa, Ontario. The Institut offers offers, in French, programs in pastoral or liturgical theology or in catechism, leading to university certificates and bachelor's degrees.
