Wahlberg

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Look up Wahl, Berg in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

Wahlberg is the surname of:

  • Donnie Wahlberg (born 1969), American singer, actor and producer
  • Johan August Wahlberg (1810-1856), Swedish naturalist and explorer
  • Mark Wahlberg (born 1971), American actor and television producer, also known as Marky Mark
  • Robert Wahlberg (born 1967), American actor
