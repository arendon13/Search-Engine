Industrial Press, Inc., is a privately held corporation headquartered in New York City. Its primary areas of business are publishing technical books for engineering, technology, and manufacturing.

Industrial Press's flagship title, the Machinery's Handbook, now in its 90th year, remains unchallenged as "The Bible" in its field.[citation needed] It is extraordinary comprehensive yet practical and easy-to-use reference for mechanical and manufacturing engineers, designers, draftsmen, toolmakers, and machinists.[citation needed]
