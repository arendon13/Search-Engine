List of Derrick episodes

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The following is an episode list for the German television series Derrick. There were a total of 281 episodes aired originally on ZDF (in Germany), ORF (in Austria) and SRG (in Switzerland) between October 20, 1974 and October 16, 1998 (25 seasons). The show was about Chief Inspector (Oberinspektor) Stephan Derrick (Horst Tappert) and his loyal assistant Inspector (Kriminalhauptmeister) Harry Klein (Fritz Wepper), who solve murder cases in Munich and surroundings.
