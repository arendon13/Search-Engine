American Guardian is a monster truck that races primarily on the USHRA Monster Jam circuit. The truck is owned by Dan Galvis from Massachusetts and driven by Rick Raab from Perkasie, PA. It is the teammate to Anger Management. The current truck is designed and built by Raab, while the first American Guardian started life out as Buffalo Tremor 3, which was owned by John Kwasniewski, and Wildfire, owned by the late Calvin Carrington. It runs primarily in the northeast and has been on the Monster Jam television show several times.
