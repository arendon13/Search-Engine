Mairangi Bay is a suburb in North Shore City in New Zealand, across the Waitemata Harbour from Auckland. For the container ship of the same name, see MV Mairangi Bay.

Mairangi Bay is under the local governance of the North Shore City Council. According to the 2001 census, Mairangi Bay has a population of 4,776.

Mairangi Bay is perhaps the closest suburb to the secondary school, Rangitoto College. 'Rangi,' as it is often called, is the largest school in New Zealand (in terms of students attending).

The Bay's beach itself has, along with adjacent Murrays and Campbells Bays, undergone civil works projects since 2004 to improve stormwater management.[1]
